The new

Porsche Sport Classic

The Porsche Sport Classic was designed to revive the spirit of Porsche's first sports cars from the 70s, combining classic aesthetics with cutting-edge modern technology. Its refined design offers a unique and exciting driving experience, which has been further enhanced over time. The Porsche Sport Classic is a true modern classic, combining the style and power of the brand's first sports cars with modern versatility and technology.

The Inspiration

Porsche 911 Carrera RS 1973

It was one of the first models from the iconic sports car brand Porsche to be built as a true race car. Designed for racing homologation, this version offered increased power, improved aerodynamics and a lighter weight. This helped to make the 911 Carrera RS one of the first models from Porsche to achieve success on the race circuits.

This was a true innovation for the Porsche racing team and helped to make the brand one of the most respected in the world of racing. By making their model a success, the 911 Carrera RS was a landmark in the history of the Porsche racing team, making it a leader in the high performance sports car industry.
